Best places to travel 2020!

Venice



Spend some quality time in the most Romantic cities in the world. With the reduction of cruise ships in the local area, the waters have transformed into crystal blue - this is the best time to visit!

Split



Now is the time to go to Croatia. With beautiful blue seas and picturesque villages, visit now to grab some September sun.

Olbia



A hidden gem on the East coast of Sardinia. Expect exotic beaches and long, warm days in this coastal regions which attracts yachts from accross the world.
